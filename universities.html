Universities & Progression

IUFP graduates progress to first-year degree study at recognised universities through formal Progression Agreements and case-by-case acceptance, subject to relevant electives and space.

ATHE Endorsed Programme — ATHE is regulated by Ofqual (UK). Certificates of Completion are issued to successful IUFP students.

Admissions & Requirements

  • Age 16+ and completed secondary school
  • Five (5) credits incl. English & Mathematics (or WAEC/NECO/IGCSE)
  • English proficiency by completion (e.g., IELTS 6.5 overall, 6.0 each)
See Overview for complete details.

Intakes, Exams & Placement

  • Intakes: January & September
  • Exams: June/July and December/January (at IUFP Partner Schools)
  • University Placement Support to guide applications
