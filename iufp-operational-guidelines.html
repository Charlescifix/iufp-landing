ATHE‑endorsed Secure Exams University Admissions

IUFP is an ATHE Endorsed Programme (Ofqual‑regulated awarding organisation).

Admissions Criteria & Application Process

ADMISSIONS CRITERIA

  • Minimum age 16; completed secondary/high school
  • 5 Credits including English and Mathematics at GCSE 'O' Level (or equivalent)

Late applications may be accepted in exceptional cases.

APPLICATION PROCESS

  • Apply online via iufp.co.uk/students or download paper form
  • Partner Schools forward completed applications to IUFP Global Head Office
  • IUFP confirms acceptance to Partner School by email

Submit by the third Friday in September for September/October starts.

Course Structure & Subjects

Six subjects completed over one academic year: 3 Core subjects (September–December) and 3 Elective subjects (January–March & April–June).

CORE SUBJECTS

  • Academic English
  • Study Skills
  • IT Applications

ELECTIVE SUBJECTS

Accounting, Biology, Business Studies, Chemistry, Computing, Economics, Fine Art, Government & Politics, Law, Mathematics, Physics, Psychology, Sociology.

Acceptance Fee & Payment

Remit at least 1 week before Partner School classes start (January/September). Confirm remittance with student names list.
