Become an IUFP Student

A one-year, intensive foundation programme that equips you for direct entry to first-year degree study—without a two-year A‑Levels route.

APPLY NOW See Universities

ATHE Endorsed Programme — ATHE is regulated by Ofqual (UK). Certificates of Completion are issued to successful IUFP students.

Fast-Track

6–12 months to prepare for first-year degree entry.

Focused

3 Core + 3 Electives mapped to your chosen degree.

Supported

Placement guidance and progress tracking every term.

Student Support

Programme Directors supervise academic progress and advise on university applications. Tutors provide ongoing feedback; progress charts are issued each term.

Award & Benchmark

On completion, students receive an IUFP Completion Certificate. Elective subjects are benchmarked on OCR specifications and aligned with UK A‑Level standards.
