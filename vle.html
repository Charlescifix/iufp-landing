VLE FAQs

Can parents/guardians view progress?

Centres can enable “observer” accounts so parents can see assignments and grades.

Is the VLE mobile‑friendly?

Yes—responsive design works on phones and tablets. Save to your home screen for quick access.

How does the AI assistant help?

It explains concepts, drafts study plans, generates quizzes, and suggests improvements to writing.
