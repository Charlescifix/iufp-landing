What admissions want to see

  • Why you want to study your chosen course (e.g., Business, Computing, Engineering). fileciteturn13file0
  • How your background, subjects and experiences support your choice. fileciteturn13file0
  • Reasons for choosing a specific university — based on location, reputation, or course strength. fileciteturn13file0
  • Why the UK, and how the degree fits your career plans. fileciteturn13file0

Pro tips

🧭
Keep it focused and specific to your course.
🔍
Reference a couple of relevant modules you’re excited about.
🧩
Connect your past (subjects/activities) to your future ambitions.
🕰️
Explain any education gaps with brief, honest context. fileciteturn13file0

Personal Statement Template

Use these prompts to draft each paragraph, then refine for clarity and flow. Adapted from the IUFP guide. fileciteturn13file0

🔑
1) Introduction

Introduce yourself and your motivation for the degree (and Foundation route, if applicable).

📚
2) Background & Motivation

Explain why you want this course and how your school subjects, projects or experiences led here.

🧪
3) Course Fit

Mention a few course modules and why they interest you; address any gaps or delays honestly. fileciteturn13file0

🏛️
4) Why University X

State your reasons (location, pedigree, course reputation) based on your research. fileciteturn13file0

🇬🇧
5) Why Study in the UK

Clarify your motivations for choosing the UK as a study destination. fileciteturn13file0

🎯
6) Career Plans

Describe your goals and how this degree will help you achieve them. fileciteturn13file0

EXAMPLE OPENING

“Design has always been my language; in secondary school I led a robotics club that turned ideas into prototypes. Studying Computer Science with AI at University X will elevate my problem‑solving from concept to impact.”

MODULE CONNECTION

“The Data Structures and Ethics in AI modules align with projects I built in Python and my interest in responsible technology.”

CAREER LINK

“I plan to specialise in human‑centred AI and contribute to health‑tech startups, where algorithmic decisions must be explainable and fair.”

Examples are illustrative; tailor to your own voice and experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should it be?

Keep it concise and compelling; focus on relevance and clarity rather than length.

Can I reuse text for multiple universities?

Keep your core story consistent, but tailor the “Why University X” paragraph to each institution. fileciteturn13file0

How do I explain a gap?

Briefly and honestly state the reason (work, travel, responsibilities) and the skills or perspective gained. fileciteturn13file0

Final Checklist

  • Course motivation is clear and authentic. fileciteturn13file0
  • University and UK reasons are specific (not generic). fileciteturn13file0
  • Career plans connect naturally to the degree. fileciteturn13file0
  • Any education gaps briefly explained with context. fileciteturn13file0
