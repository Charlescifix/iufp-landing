About IUFP

International University Foundation Programme

The IUFP is a globally recognized university pathway endorsed by ATHE (an Ofqual‑regulated awarding body), offering a tried, tested and trusted route to leading universities in the United Kingdom and beyond.

Our Mission

Bridge to University Success

We exist to bridge the gap between secondary school and university by equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive and excel in higher education. Our programme is built on academic excellence, inclusivity, innovation, and global opportunity.

Programme Structure

Two Schemes. One Standard.

  • Standard Scheme (September–August)
  • Intensive Scheme (January–September)
  • Curriculum blends Core with wide‑ranging Electives to specialise for degree pathways.

Our Network

Partner Schools Worldwide

IUFP has a vetted community of Partner Schools across Africa, Asia, Europe and the Middle East. Delivery is local in each country, with quality assured by the IUFP Global UK Team in London. Students benefit from consistent academic standards and dedicated local teaching support.
Pathway to Universities

From Foundation to Degree

Students who successfully complete the IUFP receive internationally respected transcripts and an ATHE Completion Certificate. With these, they can secure conditional or unconditional offers from a growing list of IUFP Partner Universities, many of which offer progression routes and scholarships.
