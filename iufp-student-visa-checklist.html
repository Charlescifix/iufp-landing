Documents
Applicants under 18 (before application date)
  • Letter from parent/legal guardian confirming relationship and permissions (study, living, travel).
  • Original birth certificate (or adoption certificate).
Other Considerations
Time limits & academic progression
  • All previous UK study (and time below/at degree level) must be assessed before issuing CAS.
  • If limits exceeded or progression isn't clear, do not assign a CAS.
  • For a second Masters, ensure the new programme is justified; a course director statement may be required.
