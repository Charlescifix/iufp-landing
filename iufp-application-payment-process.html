Application & Payment Process

A streamlined, step‑by‑step workflow for IUFP student applications and fee management at Partner Schools.

ATHE‑endorsedVLE‑readySecure Admissions

IUFP is an ATHE Endorsed Programme (Ofqual‑regulated awarding organisation).

STEP 1

Complete Application Form

Students complete the Paper or Online Application (via iufp.co.uk/students). Attach:

  • Recent passport photo
  • ‘O’ Level/IGCSE results — 5 Credits incl. English & Mathematics
  • IELTS (if required by progression university)
  • Passport biodata page
STEP 2

IUFP Acknowledgement

IUFP London acknowledges receipt of the completed application within 48 hours.

STEP 3

Acceptance Letter & Invoice

Successful students receive an Acceptance Letter via the Partner School contact. The letter includes the Acceptance Fee: £895.00 (excludes UCAS £28.95 & ATHE certificate £20.00).

STEP 4

Payment (within 3 working days)

Pay into the IUFP Global Limited (UK) account:

Account Name: IUFP Global Limited
Account Number: 29584868
Sort Code: 30-90-99
Bank: Lloyds Bank Plc.
BIC: LOYDGB21368
IBAN: GB94LOYD30909929584868

Payment window: up to 3 working days from invoice date.

STEP 5

Receipt & VLE Access

Upon full payment, IUFP issues a receipt and grants immediate VLE access (syllabuses, scheme of work, e‑books, past papers).

Ready to proceed?

Partner Schools can submit completed applications and remittance confirmations directly to IUFP. Students can apply online or via their Partner School, then activate VLE access once payment is confirmed.
